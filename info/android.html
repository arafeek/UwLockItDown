Setting up a password on your Android device

1) Go to Settings

2) Under Personal, navigate to security

3) Navigate to Screen Lock

Here you will be presented with different options: pattern, pin and password. Pattern is a simple connect the dots, pin is a numeric passcode and password is exactly what it sounds like.

NB: If you ever want to disable a password, navigate back to this sections and select None

4) After setting your security option, you can slo set a lock timer which will cause your device to lock itself after a set period of inactivity

5) You have secured your device and from now on will be promted to enter your password upon unlocking the screen.

Encrypting data on your Android device

WARNING please ensure you complete the following steps before attempting to encrypt your device.

1) Back-up the data on your phone before attempting to encrypt it. This is a fallback in case of data loss/corruption during the encryption process. If your phone is new, it is recommended you encrypt it before using it.

2) Have your device plugged into an external power souce with the charger during encryption. The process may take up to an hour and if the battery runs out during encryption, your device may be damaged.

3) You should set up a resonable inactivity time out to lock your device when not in use.(See Setting up a password on your Android device to see how this can be done). Encrption protects the data on the device when it is locked and even when it is off.

How to encrypt your phone:

1) Ensure your device has a lock screen PIN or password. Refer to Setting up a password on your Android device to see how.

2) Once you have a passowrd/PIN, go to settings. Navigate to "Security" and then to "Encryption". Select "Encrypt Phone".

3) Your phone will then begin the encryption process which may take an hour or more and usually requires your device to be plugged in to a charger.

4) Do not attempt to turn off your device or otherwise interrupt the encryption process as doing so could cause your data to become corrupted.
