Setting up a password on your iOS device

1) Go to Settings

2) Select General

3) Navigate to Passcode Lock

If you are using the iPhone 5s, you can also set fingerprint scanning as a security method. To do this, under General select TouchID and Passcode and then select TouchID.

Encrypting data on your iOS device

WARNING please ensure you complete the following steps before attempting to encrypt your device.

1) Back-up the data on your phone before attempting to encrypt it. This is a fallback in case of data loss/corruption during the encryption process. If your phone is new, it is recommended you encrypt it before using it.

2) Have your device plugged into an external power souce with the charger during encryption. The process may take up to an hour and if the battery runs out during encryption, your device may be damaged.

3) You should set up a resonable inactivity time out to lock your device when not in use.(See Setting up a password on your iOS device to see how this can be done). Encrption protects the data on the device when it is locked and even when it is off.

How to encrypt your phone:

1) As an iOS user, once you have a passcode lock on your device, your data is automatically encrypted. To see how to set a passcode lock refer to Setting up a password on your iOS device.
